Molecular Orbital Models

Print-ready orbital shapes for your molecular model kit. Pick a model, tune the connection hole to fit your kit's pegs and your printer, and download the STL.

Using these with your kit

These parts attach to the ball-and-stick molecular model kits you already own: each model has one or more sockets sized to slide onto your kit's pegs. Measure a peg with calipers, print one, and nudge the hole diameter up or down by 0.1 mm until the fit is snug. The diameter you set is the hole as designed. Most printers shrink holes slightly, so going a touch over your peg size is normal.
