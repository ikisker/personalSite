Searches song titles on Spotify — capped at its first 1,000 results per term. Terms match whole words only, and a title matching any term counts (“prom, homecoming” finds “Prom Queen” but not “Promise”); use regex for anything looser. Regex patterns need at least one literal word to seed the search.

    Data from Spotify. Playlists are created on the site's Spotify account — open the link and hit “Add to library” to keep one. CSV downloads import into Spotify or Apple Music via TuneMyMusic or Soundiiz.
